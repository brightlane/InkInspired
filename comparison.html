When to Use Each

Use DIY Printing When:

  • You want to maximize profit per sale
  • You're doing local/bulk orders
  • You want to build a real skill and brand
  • You need fast turnaround (same day)

Use Printful/Printify When:

  • You're testing a design idea before investing in supplies
  • You want truly passive income (no physical work)
  • You're selling 1-2 units/month in a niche

Best strategy: Use POD to test designs → switch to DIY once you know what sells.
