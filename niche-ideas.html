How to Land Your First Client in a Niche

  • Sports teams: Show up to a game with sample shirts. Coaches love it.
  • Bachelorette: Post mockups on Pinterest and Etsy — people are already searching.
  • Schools: Email the PTA president directly with a price sheet.
  • Businesses: Walk in with a branded sample made for THEM (their logo, their colors).
