Profit Strategies for Home Custom Apparel

The real money in custom apparel is in margin control. When you print yourself, you keep 60–80% of the sale price vs 10–20% with drop shipping.

Pricing Formula

Formula: (Material Cost × 2.5) + Labor

Top Revenue Streams

  • 🏆 Bulk orders — teams, schools, corporate (highest $/hour)
  • 🎉 Event shirts — bachelorettes, reunions, fundraisers
  • 🛒 Etsy shop — ongoing passive orders, low overhead
  • 📱 Local Facebook/Instagram — zero platform fees
  • 🎓 Teach workshops — $50–$100/person for printing classes
  • 🎁 Holiday bundles — Christmas, Valentine's, Mother's Day
  • 🏪 Consignment with local shops — no upfront buyer needed

First 30 Days Action Plan

  1. Order beginner kit → practice until consistent quality
  2. Make 5–10 sample pieces (your portfolio)
  3. Post samples on Instagram/Facebook with price list
  4. Reach out to 3 local sports teams or businesses
  5. Open Etsy shop with 5–10 listings
  6. Reinvest first $200 profit into a heat press

Scale Checklist

  • ☐ Consistent print quality (wash test every batch)
  • ☐ Pricing sheet ready for quick quotes
  • ☐ Turnaround time defined (standard 5 days, rush 48h)
  • ☐ Bulk discount tiers (12+, 24+, 48+ pieces)
  • ☐ Simple invoice template
  • ☐ Repeat customer follow-up system
