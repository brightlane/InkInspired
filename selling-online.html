Etsy Optimization Tips

  • Use all 13 tags per listing — include size, occasion, style keywords
  • Upload 5–7 mockup photos per listing (lifestyle photos outsell flat lays)
  • Offer personalization — it boosts Etsy algorithm ranking
  • Respond to messages within 1 hour (affects search ranking)
  • Price slightly higher than competitors — perceived value matters
  • Offer bundles: "Buy 3 get 10% off" increases average order value

Social Media Strategy (Simple Version)

  1. Post a "process video" of printing a shirt — these go viral on TikTok
  2. Show before/after: plain shirt → finished design
  3. Feature happy customer photos (ask permission)
  4. Post "would you wear this?" polls to find what designs resonate
  5. Add your affiliate or shop link to every bio

Landing Bulk Orders

One bulk order can equal weeks of individual sales. Here's how to get them:

  • Create a PDF price sheet with quantity breaks (12/24/48 pieces)
  • Offer a free sample shirt to coaches or business owners
  • Partner with a local event company or wedding planner
  • Attend small business expos and bring samples
