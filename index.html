Step-by-Step Guide to Printing at Home

1. Choose a Niche

Focus on a specific audience: fitness enthusiasts, small businesses, hobby communities. Targeted designs sell better.

2. Create Your Design

Use Canva, Photoshop, or Illustrator. Stick to bold, simple visuals for beginner screen printing success.

3. Prepare the Screen

Apply emulsion, expose your design, rinse screen to create stencil. Follow instructions in your kit for best results.

4. Print Your First Shirt

Flat shirt, align screen, apply ink evenly. Practice on scrap fabric first. DIY t-shirt printing beginners should do multiple test prints.

5. Cure the Ink

Heat curing ensures durability. Use heat press, heat gun, or oven depending on your home setup.
