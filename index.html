Step-by-Step Guide to DIY T-Shirt Printing

1. Choose a Niche

Focus on a specific audience for your t-shirts, such as hobby communities, local businesses, or fitness enthusiasts.

2. Create Your Design

Use Canva, Photoshop, or Illustrator to create beginner-friendly designs for your home-based printing business.

3. Prepare the Screen

Apply emulsion, expose design, rinse screen. Follow instructions in your home screen printing kit.

4. Print Your First Shirt

Align the screen, apply ink evenly. Practice on scrap fabric first for best results.

5. Cure the Ink

Heat curing ensures durability and quality in your custom apparel products.
